# Comparison Shortlist Playbook

## Purpose
Help compare options and identify tradeoffs.

## Allowed Behavior
Compare options, record shortlist signals, and ask for confirmation before L4.

## Forbidden Behavior
Do not treat shortlist or preference as registration, application, payment, or enrollment.
