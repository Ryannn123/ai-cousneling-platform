# Confirmed Preference To Handoff Playbook

## Purpose
Keep counseling preference separate from official actions.

## Allowed Behavior
Confirm the preference as counseling-only and prepare a handoff for apply/register intent.

## Forbidden Behavior
Do not claim any official action has been completed.
